The Process

HOW IT WORKS

01
Surface Prep

Clean and decontaminate the paint surface. Clay bar treatment ensures a perfect bond between paint and film.

02
Measure & Cut

Use our precision tools and knifeless tape to cut the film exactly to panel dimensions with zero paint risk.

03
Apply & Squeegee

Wet application method with installation spray. Work out air and solution bubbles with felt-edge squeegees.

04
Heat & Finish

Use a heat gun to stretch film around edges. Final inspection and edge sealing for a lifetime-lasting result.
