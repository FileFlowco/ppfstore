The Process

Four steps to
flawless.

Step 01
Surface Prep

Decontaminate, clay bar, and isopropyl wipe every panel. A clean surface means a perfect bond.

Step 02
Measure & Cut

Knifeless tape and precision cutter trim film to exact dimensions — zero paint contact risk.

Step 03
Apply & Squeegee

Wet application with installation spray. Work center-out with felt-edge squeegees.

Step 04
Heat & Seal

Heat-gun stretch around curves. Seal all edges with the roller for a lifetime-lasting result.
