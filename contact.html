Product & Installation
Paint Protection Film is a thermoplastic urethane film applied over your vehicle's paint. It creates an invisible barrier against rock chips, scratches, UV rays, and environmental contaminants. Our APX PPF also self-heals light scratches when exposed to heat.
Yes — with the right tools and preparation. Start with smaller panels like mirrors or door cups. The Shield Pro Kit includes a 60-page installation manual and private video library to guide you through every step.
Yes. APX PPF is available in both Gloss (enhances paint depth) and Matte (transforms gloss paint to satin finish). Select at checkout.
APX PPF carries a 10-year manufacturer warranty against delamination, yellowing, cracking, and adhesive failure. With proper care, many installations last well beyond the warranty period.
Compatible with all standard automotive paint finishes — gloss, matte, satin, and factory-wrapped surfaces. Safe for paint, plastics, chrome trim, and headlights.
