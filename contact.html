Product & Installation
Paint Protection Film (PPF) is a thermoplastic urethane film applied over your vehicle's paint. It creates an invisible barrier against rock chips, scratches, UV rays, and environmental contaminants. Our APX PPF also self-heals light scratches when exposed to heat.
Yes — with the right tools and preparation. We recommend starting with smaller panels like mirrors or door cups. The Shield Pro Kit includes a 60-page installation manual and video library access to guide you through every step.
Yes. APX PPF is available in both Gloss (transparent, enhances paint depth) and Matte (transforms any gloss paint to a satin/matte finish). Select your preferred finish at checkout.
APX PPF carries a 10-year manufacturer warranty against delamination, yellowing, cracking, and adhesive failure. With proper care and maintenance, many installations last well beyond the warranty period.
APX PPF is compatible with all standard automotive paint finishes including gloss, matte, satin, and factory-wrapped surfaces. It is safe for use on paint, plastics, chrome trim, and headlights.
